MINI FAN DISMANTLE STATION

DISASSEMBLE • SORT • RECYCLE

A smart and eco-friendly station designed to help users safely dismantle mini fans, sort electronic parts, and promote responsible e-waste recycling.

ABOUT OUR PROJECT

🌍 Vision

To create a cleaner and more sustainable future by encouraging proper e-waste management and environmental awareness through hands-on recycling activities.

🎯 Mission

The mission of the Fancycle Mini Fan Dismantle Station is to promote environmental sustainability by educating people on proper e-waste management and encouraging responsible recycling practices.

♻ Objectives

  • Promote e-waste awareness
  • Teach safe dismantling skills
  • Sort recyclable materials
  • Support sustainable recycling

👩‍💻 Developer

Developed by students as an innovation project for environmental sustainability and e-waste education.
